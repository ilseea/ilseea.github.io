Weather Hearties of SE-Idaho

Site Purpose

Making use of our pirate-theme we aim to provide younger Idahoans (our Hearties a.k.a. "friends") ... information on the climate and how it affects them directly, insight through educational weather-based articles, and knowledge of storms from sea to sea!

Our homepage will provide general weather information of the area, local towns, and of big storms around the world. You will find relevant weather-related articles, high-resolution photography of the climate, and a functioning navigational compass. And ye shant need even one dubloon for all this treasure!
