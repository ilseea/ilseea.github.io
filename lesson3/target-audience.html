Target Audience:

We are aiming to invite children ages 8-17 (with adult supervision), as well as parents, and teachers, to use our website for educational and community-based updates.

Scenarios:

  • Is the park open?
  • Where can I play with my friends when it's cold outside?
  • What trails aren't flooded?
  • What is a blizzard?
  • What do black clouds mean?
