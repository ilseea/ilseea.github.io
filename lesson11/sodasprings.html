Locusts In Farmer Larry's Corn

“Take what you can, give nothing back.”
―Fiendish Pirates


When reports of locusts rampaging through much of Africa and South Asia made headlines in Idaho, local farmers never imagined they would soon meet the fiends in their own backyards. Locusts boarded empty airplanes (left abandoned after covid-19) and made their way to America. Farmer Larry is currently fighting off a storm... by harvesting and selling locusts as a crunchy snack. Please support local farmers!

     (June 29, 2020) Article by: Nancy Drew
