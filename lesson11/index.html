Cloud Formation on the Horizon!

“I’m a pirate! I’m my own captain!”
―A Pirate Somewhere


Juanito Gonzalez was outside playing soccer, when he started feeling raindrops on his freckled nose. Stacy Rowling had scored 3 points in the last 5 minutes, and now they were tied. Juanito's concentration kept him focused on the ball. Suddenly the rain started pouring down and Stacy shouted "we gotta go! Those are Cumulonimbus clouds!". Good thing Stacy and Juanito were Weather Hearites because they knew right away that meant a powerful thunderstorm was brewing!
